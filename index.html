About Us

Shariq is the president and founder of SKS Products, an e-commerce company that offers essential and innovative products to elevate customers' lifestyles. With over three years of experience as a certified business consultant, Shariq has a passion for finding and delivering quality products that cater to various needs and preferences. He works alongside his two brothers to source the best products across the globe and provide a world-class omnichannel shopping experience.

Shariq is also a PPC and design sales specialist, with multiple certifications in digital marketing, diverse ownership, and e-commerce management. He leverages his skills and expertise to drive omnichannel sales, optimize online campaigns, and create attractive and user-friendly websites. He is always seeking new opportunities and challenges to grow his business and expand his product selection.

Shariq's vision is to create a new destination for customers to easily shop for interesting and unique items that amplify their lifestyles.
